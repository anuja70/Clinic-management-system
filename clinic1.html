About Us

Caring for Itahari Since 2005

MediCare Clinic began as a small general practice and has grown into one of Sunsari's most trusted multi-specialty hospitals, combining modern technology with heartfelt patient care.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare to every individual — treating each patient with dignity, respect, and professional excellence.

🔭

Our Vision

To become the leading healthcare institution in eastern Nepal, recognized for clinical innovation, patient safety, and continuous improvement in medical outcomes.

What We Offer

Our Medical Services

Comprehensive care under one roof — from routine checkups to complex surgeries.

General Checkup

Annual and routine health examinations with full blood work, vitals assessment, and personalized health counseling by our physicians.

Emergency Care

Round-the-clock emergency services with a dedicated trauma team, resuscitation unit, and direct ICU admission pathway.

Dental Care

Complete oral health services: cleanings, fillings, root canals, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry with digital X-ray imaging.

Laboratory

NABL-standard diagnostic lab offering 200+ tests — pathology, microbiology, biochemistry — with same-day results for most panels.

Pharmacy

On-site pharmacy stocking 5,000+ medications, operated by licensed pharmacists offering drug counseling and generic alternatives.

Pediatrics

Child-friendly environment with specialist pediatricians covering newborn care, vaccinations, growth monitoring, and adolescent health.
